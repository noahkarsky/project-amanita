Project Amanita

An endeavor to elucidate the complexities of the UFO phenomena for the public's understanding. Visualizations aim to dispel obscurity and translate the cryptic nature of the phenomena into digestible and insightful graphics

Knowledge Graph for the Phenomena

This knowledge graph was initially created to test entity extraction capabilities on the fantastic Loose Threads document, but required a detailed hand curated approach. There are two csv data files within the repo for folks who wish to do their own work with it, as well as a neo4j .dump file. The knowledge graph is a work in progress and not the most ideal tool for detailed investigation.
